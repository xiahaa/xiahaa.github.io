Dr. Xiao Hu is a Team Leader and Senior Researcher at the Lower Airspace Economy Research Institute, International Digital Economy Academy, and an Adjunct Assistant Professor at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). His research focuses on computer vision, SLAM, and visual odometry for UAV positioning and navigation systems. With expertise spanning sensor fusion, 3D reconstruction, and GNSS/GPS systems, Dr. Hu has contributed extensively to autonomous systems research through his work at leading institutions including Alibaba's DAMO Academy, DJI Innovation, and the Technical University of Denmark.
